Results

Kruskal-Wallis Test

  • fail to reject null hypothesis: both wage/salary and rent, and the price of beer are identical populations
  • heineken by rent, p-value = 0.4058
  • heineken by salary/wage, p-value = 0.4624

Spearman's rho test

  • reject null hypothesis: rho equals zero
  • heineken by rent, p-value = 0.01428, rho = 0.474675
  • heineken by salary/wage, p-value = 0.04569, rho = 0.3952097

Kendall tau rank correlation coefficient

  • reject null hypothesis of heineken price and rent are not correlated
  • heineken by rent, p-value = 0.02729, tau = 0.310565
  • heineken by salary/wage, p-value = 0.04702, tau = 0.2790782
